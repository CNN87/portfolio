About me

Hello, my name is Sinan Simsek. I'm a Jr. Front-End Developer based in Berlin, Germany. After my Fach-Abitur I studied at the Berliner Beuth Highschool for Technic (BHT) (until 2017). My favour for computers, coding and logical processes inspired me to join the Developer Akademie in Munich and become a Jr. Frontend-Web-Developer.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life, planning carefully, write clean code and good documentation. Working in a team is also a lot of fun, and I am always open to agile working methods.

In January 2023, I finally decided to turn professional and started an online coding seminar at the Developer Akademie, a professional coding school based in Munich. It has been an incredible journey until now, filled with challenges, learning, and growth. Now, I am looking forward to starting my career in IT!
